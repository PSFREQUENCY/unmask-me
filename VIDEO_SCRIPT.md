# UNMASK ME — 3-Minute Video Script

Hackathon rule: ≤3 minutes, YouTube public. Judges weight this 30/100.

Author / on-camera: **PHENOMENAL MARK** (PSFREQUENCY).

---

## Act structure

- **Act 1 (0:00 — 0:45)** — the global condition: doom-scroll, fear loops, performance-of-okay-ness, the shadow self off-camera, the corruption fatigue. Hand off into UNMASK ME as a counter-current: technology used to enhance humanity.
- **Act 2 (0:45 — 2:10)** — the product working: intake → Spirit Charm → six-protocol reset → multimodal pre/post somatic shift → adaptive Swarm → map.
- **Act 3 (2:10 — 3:00)** — the proof + the vision: open the network tab, the data does not leave the device; close on the thesis.

## Recording notes

- Phone screen capture (iPhone) at 60fps, then crop to 1080p portrait.
- ScreenStudio or QuickTime for desktop footage of DevTools.
- The voice-over should be calm, low, with breath audible — match the tone of the product. PHENOMENAL MARK's voice carries the act-one weight; do not soften it.
- Background music: nothing. Or, very low ambient drone (use `void-bell` track from the app itself for poetic continuity).
- Show the Gemma badge in EVERY scene where the app is on screen. It is the single most important UI element for judges.

## What NOT to film

- Don't show real face scans of identifiable people unless they signed consent.
- Don't show the admin secret in plaintext.
- Don't show the Gemini API key.
- Don't say "powered by Claude" or any model that isn't Gemma 4 — judges are scanning for Gemma 4 attribution specifically.
- Don't use the phrase "Soul Shine" — the brand is UNMASK ME only.
